# Privacy Policy for MCP Hub

Effective Date: 04/08/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MCP Hub ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your information when you use the MCP Hub mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner.

## **1. Introduction to MCP Hub**

The MCP Hub App is designed to connect users to various Model Contact Protocol (MCP) servers, allowing for seamless communication and data exchange within those server environments. This policy applies to data collected directly by the MCP Hub App and does not cover the data practices of third-party MCP servers you connect to, which may have their own privacy policies.

## **2. Information We Collect**

We collect both personal and technical data to provide and improve the MCP Hub App.

### **A. Personal Data You Provide Directly**

- **Account Information:** When you create an account or register to use certain features, we may collect your name, email address, and any other information you provide during the registration process.
- **Communication Data:** If you contact us for support or inquiries, we will collect the content of your communications and your contact information.
- **Profile Information:** Any information you choose to add to your user profile within the App.

### **B. Technical Data Collected Automatically**

- **Device Information:** We collect information about the device you use to access the App, including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **Usage Data:** We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as features used, screens viewed, time spent in the App, and crash reports.
- **Log Data:** When you use the App, our servers automatically record information that your device sends, including your IP address, access times, and the type of browser or client you use.
- **MCP Server Connection Data:** We collect metadata related to your connections to MCP servers, such as server addresses, connection timestamps, and connection status. We do not store the content of your communications or data exchanged on the MCP servers unless explicitly required for the App's functionality (e.g., caching for performance) and only for a limited duration.

## **3. How We Use Your Information**

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **To Provide and Maintain the App:** To operate, maintain, and improve the functionality of the MCP Hub App, including connecting to MCP servers.
- **To Personalize Your Experience:** To understand your preferences and tailor the App's features and content to you.
- **For Communication:** To send you important updates, security alerts, and support messages.
- **For Analytics and Research:** To analyze usage patterns, troubleshoot issues, and improve the App's performance and features.
- **For Security and Fraud Prevention:** To detect, prevent, and address fraudulent or illegal activity and to protect the security of our users and the App.
- **To Comply with Legal Obligations:** To meet our legal and regulatory requirements.

## **4. Data Storage, Security, and Processing**

### **A. Storage**

Your data is stored on secure servers maintained by Swagger or similar reputable service providers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

### **B. Security**

We employ a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These include:

- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit and at rest where appropriate.
- **Access Controls:** Strict access controls are in place to limit who can access your data.
- **Regular Audits:** We regularly audit our systems for vulnerabilities and potential attacks.

### **C. Processing**

Your data may be processed in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these locations.

## 5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

- **With Service Providers:** We may share data with third-party vendors and service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, customer support, and marketing. These providers are obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes for which it was disclosed.
- **For Legal Reasons:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).
- **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.
- **With Your Consent:** We may share your information with third parties when we have your explicit consent to do so.
- **MCP Server Connections:** When you connect to an MCP server, certain technical data (e.g., your IP address, device information) may be shared with that server to establish and maintain the connection. The data handling practices of the MCP server are governed by its own privacy policy.

## **6. Your Data Rights**

Depending on your location and applicable data protection laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right to Access:** You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
- **Right to Rectification:** You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
- **Right to Erasure:** You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
- **Right to Object to Processing:** You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
- **Right to Data Portability:** You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided in Section 10.

## **7. Data Handling with MCP Servers**

The MCP Hub App facilitates connections to various MCP servers. While the App itself adheres to this Privacy Policy, the data you transmit to and from an MCP server, and how that server processes your data, is governed by the privacy policy of that specific MCP server. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any MCP servers you connect to. We are not responsible for the data practices of third-party MCP servers.

## **8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies**

The MCP Hub App does not directly use cookies or similar tracking technologies for advertising purposes. However, we may use essential technical identifiers (e.g., device identifiers) for the sole purpose of providing core App functionality, such as maintaining user sessions or remembering preferences. Our third-party service providers (e.g., analytics providers) may use their own tracking technologies as described in their respective privacy policies.

## **9. Data Retention**

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. To determine the appropriate retention period, we consider the amount, nature, and sensitivity of the personal data, the potential risk of harm from unauthorized use or disclosure of your personal data, the purposes for which we process your personal data and whether we can achieve those purposes through other means, and the applicable legal requirements.

## **10. Compliance with Data Protection Laws**

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to:

- **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):** For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK.
